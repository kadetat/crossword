Crossword Creator!

Create random puzzles using your own words and your own clues, then build and play your crossword!

How to create your own crossword:

  1. Enter the title and author of your Crossword Puzzle
  2. Enter a word in the Word section
  3. Enter a clue in the Clue section
  4. Click the "Add Word" button to add additional words (Up to 30 words)
  5. Click the "Add Clue" button to add corresponding clues
  6. Click "Go!" to generate your crossword!

To see and play existing Crossword Puzzles, click on Play here or in the navigation bar.

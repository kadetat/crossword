Crossword Creator!

Create random puzzles using your own words and your own clues, then build and play your crossword!

How to create your own crossword:

  1. Enter the title and author of your new Crossword Puzzle
  2. Enter a word and a clue in the Word and Clue sections
  3. Click 'Add Word' and check out the preview
  4. Add additional words and clues (Up to 30 words)
  5. Once satisfied with your crossword, click Go! to generate your crossword!
  6. Play your new crossword online or print the crossword to solve on paper

To see and play existing Crossword Puzzles built by others, click on Play.
